## METHODOLOGY

For each entity, we ran the following discovery sequence:

1. **Primary Source Check** — every.to/team page for employee roster and roles
2. **Handle Verification** — Cross-referenced bylines, podcast transcripts, and article footers
3. **Platform Enumeration** — Searched each surface individually using `site:` and `from:` operators
4. **Pattern Matching** — Checked handle consistency across platforms (e.g., @kieranklaassen → github.com/kieranklaassen)
5. **Bio Link Archaeology** — Scraped Twitter/LinkedIn bios for personal sites, newsletters, Linktrees
6. **Product Hunt Audit** — Searched every.to product portfolio for PH presence and maker profiles
7. **Validation Rule** — A handle is "verified" only if it appears in an official Every byline, podcast transcript, or team page reference

**Confidence Levels:**
- ✅ **VERIFIED** — Handle appears in official Every content (byline, transcript, team page)
- 🔶 **LIKELY** — Handle found via search, consistent with naming pattern, but not explicitly cited by Every
- ❌ **ABSENT** — No discoverable presence on this surface
